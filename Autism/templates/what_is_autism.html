Frequently Asked Questions

Can autism be cured?
Autism is a lifelong condition, not a disease that can be "cured". With appropriate support, therapy, and intervention, individuals with autism can develop skills and lead fulfilling lives.
What causes autism?
The exact cause is not fully understood, but research suggests a combination of genetic and environmental factors.
How is autism diagnosed?
Diagnosis involves comprehensive evaluation by specialists. Screening tools like Auto-Ism can support earlier detection but do not replace medical diagnosis.
