Auto-Ism

An AI-powered early autism detection platform built to help parents and doctors identify signs as early as possible.

Our Story

We are a team of six passionate students from the Faculty of Computers and Artificial Intelligence at Cairo University (FCAI-CU). During our studies, we realized that many families in Egypt and the Arab world discover autism signs too late.

That's why we built Auto-Ism: a simple, accurate, and supportive tool that combines AI facial analysis with the SPARK questionnaire.

M

Our Mission

To empower every parent with a fast, free, and reliable way to screen for early signs of autism.

V

Our Vision

A world where no child misses the golden window of early intervention because of delayed screening.
